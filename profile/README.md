## About Our Project

**The Web Harmonium** (Web Harmonium Notes) is dedicated to making harmonium practice easy and software-free. Our main tool is a fully playable web application designed for music learners, singers, and anyone wanting to do their daily *riyaaz* straight from their desktop or mobile browser.

## What We Offer

- 🎵 **Free Online Harmonium**: A lightweight, responsive virtual instrument.
- ⌨️ **Smart Keyboard Mapping**: Play notes effortlessly using your computer keyboard.
- 🎼 **Song Notes & Guides**: Play along with guided notes (such as our popular [Arz Kiya Hai Web Harmonium Notes](https://webharmoniumnotes.com/arz-kiya-hai-web-harmonium-notes) practice page).
- 🎛️ **Full Customization**: Adjust transpose, octave, volume, and reverb on the fly to match your vocal pitch.

## Our Mission

This organization hosts the resources and community profile for the Web Harmonium project. We believe in making music education and practice tools accessible to everyone without paywalls, heavy app installations, or complicated setups. 

Just open your browser, press the keys, and let the music flow.
